# PAN, Aadhaar, TAN and Registrations

## PAN essentials

PAN is the ten-character identifier for all income-tax interactions; the fourth character encodes holder type (P individual, C company, H HUF, F firm, T trust). Apply or correct via Form 49A (49AA for foreign citizens) through Protean or UTIITSL; e-PAN with Aadhaar takes minutes. Quoting PAN is mandatory for transactions such as property purchases above ₹10 lakh, vehicle purchases, cash deposits above ₹50,000 a day, and mutual fund investments. Holding two PANs attracts a ₹10,000 penalty under Section 272B — surrender duplicates.

## PAN-Aadhaar linking

Linking is mandatory; an unlinked PAN becomes inoperative — TDS applies at higher rates under Sections 206AA/206CC, refunds are withheld, and returns face defective treatment. Link on the e-filing portal with the late fee under Section 234H. Verification status shows under Link Aadhaar Status.

## PAN verification for businesses

Bulk PAN verification is available to approved entities through the Protean online PAN verification service; standard vendor onboarding should verify PAN-name match and PAN-Aadhaar operative status to avoid higher TDS obligations and 206AB checks for non-filers (verify via the compliance check utility on the reporting portal).

## TAN

Any deductor of TDS needs a ten-character TAN (Form 49B); quoting it on challans, returns and certificates is mandatory, with a ₹10,000 penalty for failure. TAN is not needed for property TDS under 194IA (PAN-based, Form 26QB) or rent TDS under 194IB.

## GST registration workflow

Apply on the GST portal in REG-01 with PAN, Aadhaar authentication of the authorised signatory, business proof and bank details. Aadhaar-authenticated applications are approved within 7 working days absent risk flags; physical verification cases stretch to 30 days. Track via ARN; clarifications arrive in REG-03 and must be answered in REG-04 within 7 days. Amendments to core fields (legal name, principal place, partners) go through REG-14 with officer approval; non-core changes are auto-approved.
