# Annual Tax Compliance Calendar (India)

## Monthly recurring

7th: deposit of TDS/TCS deducted in the previous month. 11th: GSTR-1 for the previous month (monthly filers). 13th: GSTR-1/IFF for QRMP taxpayers (months 1-2 of quarter, optional). 20th: GSTR-3B with tax payment (monthly filers; 22nd/24th for QRMP by state). PF and ESI deposits fall on the 15th.

## Quarterly

TDS returns: 31 July (Q1), 31 October (Q2), 31 January (Q3), 31 May (Q4). Form 16A within 15 days of each return due date. Advance tax instalments: 15 June (15%), 15 September (45% cumulative), 15 December (75%), 15 March (100%). CMP-08 for composition dealers: 18th of the month after each quarter.

## Key annual dates

31 May: Q4 TDS return and Form 24Q with Annexure II. 15 June: Form 16 to employees. 31 July: ITR for non-audit individuals and firms. 30 September: tax audit report (Form 3CA/3CB-3CD). 31 October: ITR for audited entities; Form 3CEB (transfer pricing); Q2 TDS return. 30 November: ITR where transfer pricing applies. 31 December: belated/revised ITR; GSTR-9 and GSTR-9C for the previous financial year. 25 April: GSTR-4 for composition taxpayers. 30 April: March TDS deposit; GSTR-4 (where applicable).

## Company-law adjacent

AOC-4 within 30 days and MGT-7 within 60 days of the AGM (AGM by 30 September). DIR-3 KYC by 30 September. MSME half-yearly returns by 30 April and 31 October. DPT-3 by 30 June.

## Practical planning notes

September and October stack audit, TP certification, Q2 TDS and annual GST work — start reconciliations in August. ITC for a financial year lapses after the earlier of GSTR-3B for September (now 30 November window) of the following year or the annual return, so complete vendor follow-ups before that cut-off. Missing an advance-tax instalment costs 1% per month under 234C — a December check of projected income avoids most of it.
